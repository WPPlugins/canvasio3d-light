3D Plugin for WordPress - Shows easily your 3D-Models with WebGL in the format: .stl .obj .dae

== Description ==

With Canvasio3D Light can you show your 3D Models in your WordPress site. Upload your 3D-Files easily with the WordPress Media Manager on your Website in the format: .obj & mtl, .stl and .dae. - Insert a Short-Code in your side with one click on the 3D-Icon above to your WordPress Text editor. Canvasio3D Light use the 3D-Engine: three.js for WebGL.

== Installation ==

Installing the plugin takes less than one minute. 

1. Download "canvasio3d.zip" and install the plugin

2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress

3. Use the "3D"-Icon in your WordPress Text editor to insert a Short-Code for your 3D-Model
 

Compatibility: The plugin is compatible with WordPress 3.9.0 or higher.
